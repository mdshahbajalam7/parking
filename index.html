What is Parking AI?

Parking AI is a smart tool that creates parking lot designs automatically. Think of it as having an expert architect working for you 24/7. You simply upload your property plan, and the AI figures out the best way to fit parking spaces.

  • Upload your site plan (the drawing of your property)
  • AI analyzes the space and finds the best parking arrangement
  • Get a professional layout in minutes instead of days
  • Make changes easily if you need adjustments

Manage All Your Projects

Every parking design you create is saved as a "session" (like a project folder). You can come back anytime to view, edit, or share your designs.

  • All your parking projects are saved automatically
  • Open old projects to make changes or create new versions
  • Delete projects you no longer need
  • Everything is organized in one easy-to-use dashboard

Simple File Upload

Getting started is easy. Just drag your site plan file into the upload area, or click to browse your computer. The system accepts standard CAD files that architects and engineers use.

  • Supports DXF and DWG files (common design file types)
  • Files up to 100 MB are accepted
  • Drag and drop or click to upload - whatever you prefer
  • You'll see a confirmation message when your file is ready

AI Guides You Through Setup

The AI doesn't just create designs - it helps you make smart choices too. Answer a few simple questions about what you need, and the AI takes care of the rest.

  • Step-by-step guidance through the design process
  • Choose your building location and parking preferences
  • Use quick presets for common parking types (saves even more time)
  • AI suggests the best options based on your site

See Your Parking Layout Instantly

Once the AI creates your design, you can see everything clearly on screen. Different colors help you understand each part of the parking lot at a glance.

  • Your parking layout appears in the center of the screen
  • Colors show different areas: parking spaces, driving lanes, and restricted zones
  • Easy to review and check if the design works for your needs
  • Zoom in and out to see details or the big picture
