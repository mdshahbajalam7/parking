What is Parking AI?

Parking AI is a smart tool that creates parking lot designs automatically. Think of it as having an expert architect working for you 24/7. You simply upload your property plan, and the AI figures out the best way to fit parking spaces.

  • Upload your site plan (the drawing of your property)
  • AI analyzes the space and finds the best parking arrangement
  • Get a professional layout in minutes instead of days
  • Make changes easily if you need adjustments
  • Export designs in industry-standard formats

Manage All Your Projects

Every parking design you create is saved as a "session" (like a project folder). You can come back anytime to view, edit, or share your designs.

  • All your parking projects are saved automatically in the cloud
  • Open old projects to make changes or create new versions
  • Delete projects you no longer need with one click
  • Everything is organized in one easy-to-use dashboard
  • Search and filter projects by name, date, or location

Simple File Upload

Getting started is easy. Just drag your site plan file into the upload area, or click to browse your computer. The system accepts standard CAD files that architects and engineers use.

  • Supports DXF and DWG files (common design file types used worldwide)
  • Files up to 100 MB are accepted - handle even complex site plans
  • Drag and drop or click to upload - whatever you prefer
  • You'll see a confirmation message when your file is ready
  • Instant file validation to ensure compatibility
